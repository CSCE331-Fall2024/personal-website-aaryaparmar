Qualifications

My technical skills span a wide range of programming languages, development tools, and methodologies, reflecting a solid foundation in both software and data engineering. I am proficient in languages such as Java, Python, SQL, and C++, which have allowed me to tackle complex programming challenges and develop robust, efficient solutions. My experience with Azure Synapse, PySpark, and cloud computing platforms has further strengthened my ability to work with large-scale data, ensuring that I can handle everything from data ingestion to analysis and reporting. Additionally, I am skilled in web development technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabling me to create dynamic, user-friendly interfaces and applications.

I have an interest in data science, health tech, and artificial intelligence (AI). My fascination with data science stems from its ability to uncover patterns and insights from vast amounts of information, which can be leveraged to solve complex problems across various industries. I am particularly drawn to the intersection of data science and health tech, where I see enormous potential for improving healthcare outcomes through data-driven decision-making. Projects like my Yoga Schedule Builder app reflect this interest, as they combine my passion for health tech with my technical skills in data processing and user interface design.

AI is another area that deeply intrigues me due to its transformative impact on technology and society. The ability of AI to learn, adapt, and solve problems autonomously opens up endless possibilities for innovation. I am eager to explore how AI can be integrated with data science and health tech to develop intelligent systems that enhance human well-being and efficiency. Whether it's using AI to predict health trends or developing algorithms that drive financial literacy tools, I am passionate about harnessing the power of these technologies to create solutions that make a real difference.
